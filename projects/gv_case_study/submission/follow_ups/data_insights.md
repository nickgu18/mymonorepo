# What other insights can you glean from the data that you have extracted?

Some ideas that were not explored due to time constraints:


- **Time Period**: Macro economic trends likes interest rates and inflation could affect the success of a startup. And can last for a long time before changes. These are not directly observable from the data, but we can augment the dataset with macroeconomic data since we know the year of the company's founding. *note* that we might also need to know the exit year of the company to know the effect of macroeconomic trends.
- **Industry Trends**: Analysis of founding dates and industries can show which sectors are heating up or cooling down. That being said, there are only 3 industries in the dataset. if there are more industries, we can do industry trend analysis.
- **Team Composition**: Analyzing the mix of roles (e.g., CEO + CTO vs. solo founder) and their impact on success. I already explored their various role counts and network quality, this can be expanded to include more roles and network quality (i.e 2nd degree qualtiy of their teammates/co-founders)
- **Career Trajectories**: Common paths taken by successful founders. I.e previous roles and companies, number of years at small/mid/large company, etc. All of these require augmentation but can be expanded.
