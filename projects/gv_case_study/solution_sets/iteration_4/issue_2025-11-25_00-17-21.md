### analysis

The current notebook does not yet implement the four-part story in
`solution_sets/iteration_4/changes.md`:

1. It does not explicitly define the training unit as a **single founder
   snapshot at `label_company_founded_year`** tied to a chosen label company.
2. It does not build an explicit **pre-split founder matrix** with
   `person_id`, `label_max_founder_multiple`, `label_company_id`,
   `label_company_founded_year`, and `label_company_industry` before any
   train/val/test split.
3. Its temporal split is not expressed as “sort by `label_company_founded_year`
   within each industry and then take 70% / 15% / 15% founders in time order”.
4. It does not clearly separate **pre-split local features** from
   **post-split, train-fitted features** (performance aggregates,
   network features, and team features).

In other words, the gap is not a bug in the math, but that the notebook flow
still reflects an older “monolithic training dataset” design instead of the
clean, time-respecting pipeline described in `changes.md`:

- One row per founder.
- A label company chosen as the **best exit (max multiple)**.
- An explicit `label_company_founded_year` that anchors all features.
- A pre-split founder matrix with only local, non–split-aware features.
- A temporal train/val/test split by industry and `label_company_founded_year`.
- Post-split additions of performance, network, and team features that are
  fit on train only and applied to val/test.
